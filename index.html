Mid-Day-Meal

The Mid-Day Meal Scheme is the scheme started by West Bengal government for providing better nutrition for children and improving education, attendance, learning levels of children and encouraging poor children to attend school regularly. This scheme is helping to poor children in getting nutrition at free of cost.
